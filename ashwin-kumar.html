Studio & Creative Director, Founder of Kleem Productions

Ashwin Kumar

Ashwin Kumar is a distinguished filmmaker, visual storyteller, and creative visionary whose work seamlessly blends art, technology, and spirituality. As the Director of the animated feature film Mahavatar Narsimha, he has redefined Indian animation cinema through his unique visual language and narrative depth. The film has earned global recognition, winning multiple accolades including Best Animated Feature, Outstanding Visual Production, Best Screenplay at international film festivals. Under Ashwin’s direction, Mahavatar Narsimha stands as a landmark in Indian animation — a magnificent synthesis of devotion, innovation, and cinematic brilliance.

With nearly two decades of experience in the creative industry, Ashwin’s journey has been as inspiring as his films. A seasoned Creative Visualizer and Art Director, his repertoire spans over 50+ ad films, independent features, and mainstream cinema.

Ashwin’s creative pursuits are rooted in his entrepreneurial vision. As the co-founder of Kleem Productions, alongside producer Shilpaa Dhawan, he has built a brand that embodies the philosophy “Where Creativity Meets Divinity.” Under his leadership, Kleem Productions has grown into a powerhouse of artistic innovation, producing visually breathtaking content that bridges spirituality, devotion, technology, and cinematic excellence.

His work extends beyond traditional filmmaking. Ashwin created the groundbreaking VR short film Sakshaat Darshan, an immersive experience that allowed viewers to encounter divinity virtually. Premiered during Janmashtami at ISKCON Noida, it was witnessed by thousands in a single day — a testament to his pioneering approach to spiritual storytelling.

Having worked with industry giants such as Eros International and Pixion Studios, Ashwin has contributed to acclaimed films like Hari Puttar, Jaane Tu Ya Jaane Na, No Smoking, Sunday, and Drona. His creative portfolio also includes campaigns for brands like McDonald’s, Lenovo, Maruti, Tata AIG, Aditya Birla, and Relco Tyres, among others.

A rare combination of artist, innovator, and leader, Ashwin Kumar continues to inspire the future of Indian animation and cinema — transforming history and spirituality into motion, and imagination into timeless art.
