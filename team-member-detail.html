Director . Producer

Ashwin Kumar

Ashwin Kumar is the director of the recently successful animated mythological film, Mahavatar Narsimha, a groundbreaking project that aimed to elevate Indian animation. A dedicated filmmaker with a background in entrepreneurship, advertising, and VFX, Kumar founded Kleem VFX Studio and has contributed to various Bollywood films and advertising campaigns.

His vision for Mahavatar Narsimha was to create a realistic, culturally significant animated film, a goal he achieved with the movie becoming a box-office hit and a significant moment for Sanatan Sanskriti on the big screen
