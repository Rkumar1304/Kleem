Managing Director & Founder, Kleem Productions

Shilpaa Dhawan

With over eighteen years of multifaceted experience spanning management consulting, entrepreneurship, and creative production, Shilpaa Dhawan is a visionary business leader and a pioneering film producer. As the Founder and Managing Director of Kleem Productions, she has been instrumental in shaping the studio’s creative and strategic direction, establishing it as a powerhouse of innovation in the Indian animation and visual storytelling space.

Her debut animated feature film, Mahavatar Narsimha, produced under the Kleem Productions banner, has garnered widespread international acclaim for its extraordinary visual effects, spiritual depth, and authentic portrayal of Vedic heritage. The film has received multiple accolades, including Best Animated Feature, Outstanding Visual Production, at renowned international film festivals — positioning Shilpaa as a trailblazer in India’s animation genre.

Shilpaa’s professional journey began in London, where she joined a leading fashion modelling company and rapidly rose through leadership roles within just four years. Returning to India in 2010, she transformed her family’s corrugated packaging manufacturing business by modernizing its operations and doubling turnover within two years. Over the next decade, she collaborated with startups, global multinationals, and turnaround ventures — consistently driving revenue growth, operational excellence, and strategic transformation.

In 2018, she founded Kleem Productions, a creative venture focused on producing animation films inspired by the Vedic Shastras, further expanding her vision to blend spirituality with cinematic innovation.

An alumna of Amity Business School, Noida, with a BBA, Shilpaa exemplifies the perfect balance of business intellect and creative intuition. Her unwavering belief in India’s rich spiritual legacy continues to inspire her mission — to bring ancient wisdom to modern audiences through art, animation, and storytelling. A true force to reckon with, Shilpaa Dhawan continues to transform divine ideas into timeless cinematic experiences under the banner of Kleem Productions.
