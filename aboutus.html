The Beginning

Kleem Productions was born from a simple yet profound vision — to unite authentic storytelling with the artistry of world-class filmmaking.

First Big Step

Our debut short film premiered at a regional film festival, receiving acclaim for its originality and striking visuals.

Building the Team

We grew into a full-scale production house, welcoming skilled directors, animators, and VFX artists to bring our vision to life.

Recognition Arrives

Partnered with international collaborators on acclaimed projects that solidified our place as rising voices in contemporary cinema.

Expanding Horizons

Stepped into large-scale productions, seamlessly integrating live-action with cutting-edge VFX and animation.

Reaching Global Audiences

Our films started captivating international festivals, streaming platforms, and viewers worldwide, marking our debut on the global stage.

The Journey Continues

With every project, we strive to push creative and technological boundaries, crafting stories that inspire and endure across generations.

Our Vision

To create a global cinematic universe inspired by the timeless wisdom of Sanatana Dharma, delivering visually stunning and emotionally resonant films that evoke awe, reflection, and a deeper connection to our heritage.

Our Mission

To combine masterful storytelling with cutting-edge animation and VFX, producing authentic, high-quality epics that captivate audiences worldwide and stand as a cultural beacon for generations.
